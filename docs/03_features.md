# Features by Role

## 1. Client Features

### Authentication & Onboarding
- **Registration**: Sign up with email, phone, and personal details.
- **Login**: Secure authentication with JWT tokens.
- **OTP Verification**: Phone number verification.
- **Guest Mode**: Explore the app without an account.

### Discovery & Ordering
- **Nearby Restaurants**: Geolocation-based discovery.
- **Search**: Real-time search for restaurants and items.
- **Filtering**: Filter by category, price, and rating.
- **Menu Browsing**: View items with details, sizes, and toppings.
- **Cart**: Manage items, apply coupons, and checkout.

### Payment & Tracking
- **Paymob Integration**: Secure online payments.
- **Order Tracking**: Real-time status updates (Pending, Preparing, Out for Delivery, Delivered).
- **History**: View past orders and reorder favorites.

### Profile Management
- **Addresses**: Save multiple delivery addresses with map integration.
- **Favorites**: Save favorite restaurants.
- **Settings**: Theme (Dark/Light) and Language (English/Arabic) preferences.

---

## 2. Restaurant Owner Features

### Dashboard
- **Overview**: View total orders, revenue, and active items.
- **Charts**: Visual performance metrics.

### Menu Management
- **Items**: Add, edit, delete, and toggle availability of menu items.
- **Categories**: Organize items into categories.

### Order Management
- **Incoming Orders**: Accept or reject new orders.
- **Status Updates**: Mark orders as Preparing or Ready for Pickup.
- **History**: View completed orders.

### Profile
- **Restaurant Info**: Update name, description, hours, and contact info.
- **Reviews**: Monitor customer feedback.

---

## 3. Delivery Agent Features

### Order Fulfillment
- **Available Orders**: View and accept delivery assignments.
- **Active Deliveries**: Track ongoing deliveries with customer and restaurant details.
- **Status Updates**: Mark orders as Out for Delivery or Delivered.

### Tracking & Earnings
- **Navigation**: Integration with maps for route guidance.
- **Earnings**: Track delivery income.
- **History**: View completed deliveries.

---

## 4. Admin Features

### Platform Management
- **Dashboard**: High-level metrics (users, restaurants, orders, revenue).
- **Users**: Manage customer accounts (ban/unban).
- **Restaurants**: Approve, edit, or disable restaurants.
- **Agents**: Manage delivery agent accounts and assignments.

### Content Management
- **Coupons**: Create and manage discount codes.
- **Sliders**: Manage promotional banners.
- **Notifications**: Send push notifications to users.
- **Categories**: Manage food categories and subcategories.
